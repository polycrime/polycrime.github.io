Modulate is a first person puzzle game about discovery in unknown spaces. Players have the ability to toggle objects on and off and slowly discover the depth of this behavior as well as the story of the worlds. This game is about treating players with respect and a hate letter to many modern games. It contains no instructions or guidance as to how to play. Everything must be explored -- from behaviors to narrative threads.
